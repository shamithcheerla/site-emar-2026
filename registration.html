Format Requirements

Papers must be submitted in PDF format only (max 10MB). Please adhere to the IEEE double-column format. Length: 4-6 pages (no page numbers).

Plagiarism Policy

Zero-tolerance policy. Checked via Turnitin. Similarity index must be <15%. No self-plagiarism.

Review Process

Double-blind peer review by at least two independent reviewers. Criteria: originality, quality, relevance, and clarity.
